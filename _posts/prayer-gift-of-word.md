Heavenly Father,  
We thank you for the gift of your Word.  
What we have learned, help us to remember.  
What we remember, help us to obey.  
And what we obey, help us to reflect in our lives,  
that Christ may be seen in us, through Jesus Christ our Lord.

Amen.
